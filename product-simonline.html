SimulationOnline

An outcome from pet projects during COVID, my internal startup resulted in a company-wide paradigm shift, new product lines, and 10+ new client projects and products that I managed.

Product overview

  • Start date: 01.06.2020

  • Launches: 5 clients, 7 product launches

  • Company: VirtaMed

  • Clients: Bayer, CooperSurgical, Aspivix, Momentis, Teleflex

  • Team: 1-6 members (depending on client)

  • Award: Best Product Engineer

  • Skills and tools used: Agile product development, roadmap planning and execution, SaaS, web-based products, Unity WebGL, Google Analytics, Power Bi, Learning Management Systems, Jira/Confluence, international and multidisciplinary teams.

Product details

The focus of SimulationOnline is to leverage the simulator assets we already have and provide accessible surgical skills with a higher recurring revenue business. SimulationOnline allows for didactic and interactive skills training online from anywhere, anytime and most devices that can connect to the internet.
Over the past almost 4 years, I released 7 products for 5 clients. Here are some live examples available today: CooperSurgical IUS insertion and Teleflex AC3 Optimus troubleshooting.
